About Me

Hi, I'm Muhammad Arham Jamil, a passionate Web Developer, Full Stack Enthusiast, and an Associate Teacher for A-level Computer Science at Whales College. With over two years of experience in creating visually appealing, user-friendly interfaces, I specialize in front-end technologies and am always expanding my skills in back-end development to build scalable, robust web applications.

My journey into web development began with an eagerness to explore the potential of computers and the joy of crafting solutions from code. Having completed my A-levels in Computer Science, I currently balance my passion for development with teaching, inspiring and mentoring students while staying up-to-date with the latest trends in the tech world.

I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, driven by curiosity and the thrill of problem-solving. I am committed to writing optimized and efficient code while ensuring that the projects I work on are modern, innovative, and adhere to the best practices in the industry.
